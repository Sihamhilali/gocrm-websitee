About Us

About GoCRM

We're on a mission to make powerful business software accessible to every company, everywhere. Here's the story behind GoCRM.

Our Mission

Our mission is to democratize business software. We believe every business, regardless of size, deserves access to powerful tools that help them compete and thrive in the digital age.

Our Story

GoCRM was born from a simple observation: small and medium businesses were paying enterprise prices for basic CRM and ERP tools — or worse, struggling with spreadsheets and disconnected apps.

Our founders, veterans of the SaaS industry, set out to build an all-in-one platform that combines CRM, ERP, project management, and analytics at a fraction of the cost.

Today, GoCRM serves thousands of businesses across 30+ countries, and we're just getting started. Every day, we work to make our platform more powerful, more intuitive, and more affordable.
