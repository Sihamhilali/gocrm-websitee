About GoCRM

We're building the future of business management software.

Our Mission

Our mission is to democratize business software. We believe every business, regardless of size, deserves access to powerful tools that help them compete and thrive in the digital age.

GoCRM was born from a simple observation: small and medium businesses were paying enterprise prices for basic CRM and ERP tools — or worse, struggling with spreadsheets and disconnected apps. We set out to change that. Founded in 2021, GoCRM has grown from a small startup to a trusted platform used by over 5,000 businesses across 30+ countries. Our team of 50+ passionate builders is committed to making business software that's powerful, beautiful, and accessible to everyone.
